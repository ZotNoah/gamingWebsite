About Me

Hello, and welcome to my Webiste!

My name is Noah Unverzagt, and I am a college student pursuing a degree in Web Development at Metropolitan Community College in Omaha, Nebraska. My objective is to obtain an entry-level position as a Front-End Developer, where I can apply my skills and continue to grow professionally.

I am currently employed at Nebraska Furniture Mart while completing my studies. Most of my web development experience has come through academic projects, but I have also completed two internships that have broadened my practical knowledge of the field.

The first was with the Childhood Cancer Society, where I gained valuable experience in the design process and learned how to collaborate effectively with a team to bring a shared vision to life. The second was with Nebraska Furniture Mart, where I was introduced to Agile development practices, the fundamentals of SQL, and backend technologies such as ASP.NET.

I am passionate about building user-friendly, accessible websites and am eager to contribute to a professional development team.
